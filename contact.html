What is your mission?

To support and promote community led Conservation initiatives for the sustainable management and restoration of Kirisia Forest as a gateway for an improved standards of living and socio-economic transformation of the communities and residents of Samburu County.

Can I volunteer?

Yes, we welcome volunteers! Please visit our Volunteer page to see possibilities and join up for events and activities that align with your interests and availability.
