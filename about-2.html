Join Us in Conserving Kirisia Forest

A Community-Driven Conservation Effort

Kirisia CFA is a dedicated Community Forest Association (CFA) working to protect, restore, and sustainably manage Kirisia Forest. By engaging local communities, we promote biodiversity conservation, climate resilience, and eco-friendly livelihoods.

Our Mission

Our Daily Activities

"To support and promote community-led conservation initiatives for the sustainable management and restoration of Kirisia Forest as a gateway to improved standards of living and socio-economic transformation for the communities of Samburu County."

Our Vision

Our Long term objectives

"To be a leader in community-based environmental and biodiversity conservation for Kirisia Forest and its environs."

Dedicated to Conservation and Community Empowerment

At Kirisia Community Forest Association (K-CFA), we are committed to restoring and conserving the Kirisia Forest ecosystem while uplifting the livelihoods of the local communities. Through collaborative conservation efforts, sustainable livelihood programs, and community-driven initiatives, we strive to protect our natural heritage for future generations.
