Overview

Kirisia Community Forest Association (K-CFA) is dedicated to improving the livelihoods of local communities while ensuring the sustainable management of Kirisia Forest. Many residents rely on forest resources for survival, making economic empowerment and alternative livelihood programs essential for long-term conservation efforts. Through skills training, sustainable projects, and community engagement, K-CFA is helping residents transition to eco-friendly economic activities that reduce dependency on forest resources.

Key Initiatives
  • ✅ Beekeeping programs to provide alternative income sources 🐝
  • ✅ Agroforestry & tree nurseries for economic & environmental sustainability 🌱
  • ✅ Poultry farming initiatives to support food security 🐔
  • ✅ Microbusiness funding for former charcoal burners to start eco-friendly businesses 💰
  • ✅ Community training & education on sustainable forest use 📚
Impact
  • 600,000+ KES disbursed to support community-led businesses.
  • Hundreds of beekeeping hives set up for honey production & income generation.
  • Community-run tree nurseries supplying seedlings for forest restoration.
  • Reduced pressure on Kirisia Forest by offering alternative livelihoods.
"Every effort, from reforestation to community stewardship, plays a vital role in protecting Kirisia Forest for future generations." 🌿

Partner With Us

We invite organizations, conservation groups, and stakeholders to join us in our mission to protect and restore Kirisia Forest. Through strategic partnerships, we can amplify our impact, strengthen community-led conservation, and ensure sustainable forest management for future generations.

By working together, we can:

  • ✅ Enhance reforestation efforts and expand tree planting programs.
  • ✅ Support forest patrols and community ranger initiatives.
  • ✅ Develop eco-friendly livelihood programs to reduce forest dependence.
  • ✅ Promote education and awareness on biodiversity conservation.

We are proud to collaborate with dedicated partners like Kenya Forest Service (KFS), FAO, Suyian Trust, and ACTED Kenya, who play a vital role in sustaining our conservation initiatives. 🌿 Join us in safeguarding Kirisia Forest! Reach out today and explore how we can work together to create a lasting environmental impact.
